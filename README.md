## Projects

### 01 — Global Sustainability and Happiness Analysis

Exploratory analysis of 140 countries using the Global Sustainability and Peace Index dataset. Investigates what actually drives national happiness — finding that governance quality and human development are stronger predictors than GDP alone.

**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, NumPy  
**File:** 01-Global_Sustainability_Analysis.ipynb

Key findings:
- GDP and happiness follow a logarithmic relationship — gains flatten above approximately USD 20,000 per capita
- HDI correlates more strongly with happiness (r = 0.80) than GDP alone
- Governance quality (Corruption Index, r = 0.74) is an independent predictor of wellbeing
- Europe leads on both happiness and lowest pollution; Africa and Asia face a compounding disadvantage on both measures

### 02 — Supermarket Sales Analysis

End-to-end retail analysis of 1,000 transactions across three branches, combining Python visualisation with SQL queries in a single workflow. Covers branch performance, product profitability, customer behaviour, and revenue trends.

**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, SQL (SQLite)  
**File:** 02-Supermarket_Sales_Analysis.ipynb

Key findings:
- All three branches perform within 3% of each other in total revenue
- Food and Beverages and Sports and Travel are the highest revenue product lines
- Member and Normal customer revenue is split almost equally with similar basket sizes
- February shows a revenue dip — indicating potential for mid-quarter promotions
